Minimap & Backpack

Never get lost again with Xaero's Minimap, and dramatically expand your inventory with a wearable Traveler's Backpack!

How to install:
1. Open the Modrinth App and click on your Donut Plains instance.
2. Click Add Content (the puzzle piece icon).
3. Search for Xaero's Minimap and click Install.
4. Search for Traveler's Backpack and click Install.

Shaders & Performance

Want beautiful lighting and shadows? You'll need the foundational shader mods for Forge to make it happen!

How to install:
1. In the Modrinth App, click Add Content.
2. Search for Oculus and click Install (this allows you to load shaders).
3. Search for Embeddium and click Install (this is the required performance engine for Oculus).

📷 [Drop 'installing_shaders_step_1.gif' in the /images folder]

4. Finally, download our official shader pack and drop the ZIP file into your instance's Shaderpacks folder!
